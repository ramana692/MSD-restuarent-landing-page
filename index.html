Our Story

Founded in 2010 by chef Michael Waters, The Coastal Bistro brings the taste of the ocean to your table. What began as a small seaside eatery has grown into an award-winning destination for seafood lovers.

We pride ourselves on sustainable sourcing, working directly with local fishermen and farmers to bring you the freshest ingredients. Our menu changes with the seasons to showcase nature's bounty at its peak.

More than just a restaurant, we're a community gathering place where memories are made over shared plates and stunning sunsets. Our warm, nautical-inspired decor and friendly service will make you feel like you're dining at your favorite coastal retreat.
